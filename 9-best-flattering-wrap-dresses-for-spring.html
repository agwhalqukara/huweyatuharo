9 best flattering wrap dresses for spring

A wrap dress is a must-have staple for any wardrobe...

    Wrap dresses are a trend that will never go out of style, and we can see why. The right wrap dress can be dressed up or down for just about any occasion, and they're super flattering on any body type.

    Plenty of celebs and royals have been seen in stylish wrap designs, from Kate Middleton’s array of floral frocks to Meghan Markle’s iconic Oprah dress. Whether you’re on the hunt for a pretty daywear wrap dress or a flattering midi for wedding season, scroll the edit of the best wrap dresses you need for spring.
