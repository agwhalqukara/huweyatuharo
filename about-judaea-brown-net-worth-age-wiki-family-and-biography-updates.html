About Judaea Brown: Net Worth, Age, Wiki, Family, and Biography Updates


Judaea Brown is an American actress, known for her work in movies, television shows, print advertisements, voiceovers, and TV commercials. She rose to fame for her role as Jemma St. John in the TV series The Chi.

Judaea Brown’s Biography

Judaea, also known as JB, is a Taekwondo and dance instructor. In addition to her on-screen acting, she has multiple Broadway credits and enjoys tennis, soccer, biking, and modeling in her spare time.

She is a trained dancer with Steve Southall/Donna Brum Dancers and American Dance, with expertise in tap, hip hop, and ballet dancing.

Judaea Brown’s Age, Height, and Weight

Judaea was born on 12 August 2001, making her 21 years old. She stands at 5 feet 1 inch (1.54 m) and weighs 47 kg (103 lbs).

Judaea Brown’s Career

Brown gained popularity as Jemma in The Chi in 2020. She started her career at the age of five, with work in voiceovers, commercials, and print advertisements. Her portfolio includes ads for companies such as WOW Cable, Jim’s Gym, Vitamix, Kraft Foods, Inc, Build A Bear, and Steak & Shake.

Brown has also performed in South Pacific on the National Broadway Tour, Macbeth at the Lyric Opera House of Chicago, Margaret Garner Opera at the Auditorium Theatre, and the Christmas Story on Broadway. She has been represented by Gray Talent Group since she was eight years old.
